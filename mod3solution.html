Chicken

A source of protein low in fat and high in protein. It is eaten by most people throughout the world. Various recipes have been developed using this protein.

Beef

Beef is a good source of protein which comes from cows. Many people in the world eat beef, especially in the form of a patty for hamburgers or produced in another way such as steak.

Sushi

Sushi contains good fats and is high in protein. It covers a broad range of types such as salmon, tuna, etc. Many people prefer fish to other protein sources.
